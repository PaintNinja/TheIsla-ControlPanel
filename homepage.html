Get to CP stuff without even opening the CP

This is a quick and easy trick that'll let you access stuff such as the console, server dashboard, community website, forums, tutorials and just about everything the CP has without even opening the CP!

All you have to do is right click the island icon rather than left clicking it. Once you've done that, you'll have a list just like the menu on the left in the control panel.
